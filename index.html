Effects of Covid-19 on Aspiring Athletes

We are team of talented students

When young athletes had their routine disrupted, as occurred during the COVID-19 pandemic,

They were at greater risk for depression, demonstrating the need for increased education and research in mental health for adolescent athletes. The results of a survey, “Sidelined due to COVID-19: Youth Athletes Sleep More, Practice Less, and May Lose Interest in Playing Sports Due to Social Distancing Mandates,” presented at the 2021 Annual Meeting of the American academy of orthopedic surgeons(AAOS) just shows how crucial the need for awareness on this topic is.
